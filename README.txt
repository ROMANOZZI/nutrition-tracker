How to run the program?
-Download the rar and extract it 
-go to nutrition tracker--2\dist and run nutrition tracker--2.jar
how does it work?
-first you enter your data(gender,age,weight,Activity,"whether you are diabetic or not" and press submit
- if the data is valid that take you to the second page which shows you your bodyshape , the food list, the calories counter,the hydration counter 
_the calories counter add the calories of the type you added from the food lis
-the calories counter reset automatically after 24hrs
-the hydration minimum act dynamically with the counter every time you add a cup
-the hydrattion minimum is calculated based on your data
-hydration counter reset every 24hrs
-the food list is filtered if you are diabetic based on the glycemic index
